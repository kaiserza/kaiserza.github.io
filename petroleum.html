OIL
The basic material in HID's prox cards, which is what MSU's ID cards are, is PVC -- Polyvinyl Chloride, a petrochemical. PVC begins its life as a byproduct during the oil refining process.
SILICON WAFERS AND INTEGRATED CIRCUITS
It's nearly impossible to figure out what type of chip/integrated circuit is inside a given HID smart/prox card. At the same time, it's likely that it's a chip using the ARM architecture. While the architecture/design belongs to ARM, the IP is liecensed to various manufacturers all over the world, including, probably most prominently, Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company.
